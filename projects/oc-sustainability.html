OC Sustainability Decathlon 2023 - Team Roots

Objective & Motivation

This project involved executing the physical implementation of a sustainable residential design developed by an interdisciplinary architecture team for the Orange County Sustainability Decathlon. My role focused on translating architectural intent into real-world execution under strict budget, time, and logistical constraints. The project placed 2nd among 12 universities in the Architecture & Interior Design category.

Key Contributions

  • Interpreted architectural design intent into furniture specifications, spatial layout, and procurement decisions
  • Sourced furnishings to match design requirements while reducing total project costs by $8,000 through sponsorship
  • Led a four-person team to complete full house staging in under two days under fixed exhibition deadlines
