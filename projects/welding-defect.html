Autonomous Welding Defect Detection — Concept Design (Hackathon)

Objective & Motivation

This project was developed during an AI-focused hackathon to address safety and quality challenges in industrial welding. The motivation arose from an aging skilled welding workforce and high injury rates associated with manual welding operations. The proposed system explores how autonomous, perception-based inspection could reduce operator risk while improving weld quality consistency.

System Concept

The proposed system integrates camera and X-ray sensing at multiple welding stations to enable real-time defect detection and quality assessment. Inspection data is aggregated by a centralized model to predict weld integrity, flag anomalies, and provide feedback to operators and downstream automation systems.
