Research

Imitation Learning Model for Day–Night Autonomous Driving

Using RGB and thermal vision, an imitation learning model was created and tested on a go-kart to provide low-level steering and throttle control in a variety of lighting scenarios.

Defense-in-Depth Security Architecture for CAN-Based Autonomous Vehicle Control Systems

In order to validate a defense-in-depth security architecture, real-world CAN bus attack scenarios, including as message injection and replay, were carried out from an external laptop to override steering and throttle on a physical autonomous vehicle.

Engineering Projects

Base Isolator Study

Conducted comparative analysis of seismic base isolation systems, investigating structural dynamics and vibration mitigation techniques for earthquake-resistant building design.

Mini-Fridge Design Concept

Designed a preliminary concept for a compact 12×12 mini-fridge based on a reversed Brayton cycle.

Applied Execution

Autonomous Welding Defect Detection — Concept Design (Hackathon)

Developed and led a system-level concept for autonomous welding detection using perception-based inspection, addressing safety and quality challenges in industrial welding. (2nd place, AI for a Better Future category).
